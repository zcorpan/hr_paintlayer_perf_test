Discover a new menu everyday

Each day we send you a short menu of carefully chosen dishes from local lunch spots. We offer different cuisine types throughout the week and introduce new restaurants and dishes all the time. Your lunch routine just got a whole lot less routine.

Relax and enjoy your lunch

We deliver lunches to your office's front desk around noon. We'll text you when it arrives, but you can pick it up whenever you're ready. Use the time you save by avoiding long lines to actually enjoy lunch. Make your lunch break, a break.
